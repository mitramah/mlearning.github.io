Challenges In Mobile Learning

Technical challenges for M-learning includes:

  • Connectivity and battery life.
  • Screen size and the key size (Alshahrani, Ally, 2016).
  • Limited data storage.
  • Risk of sudden obsolescence. (Crescente and Lee, and Elias, 2011).
  • The trouble of rewriting the existing E-learning materials to make them mobile compatible.
  • Online streaming required particular bandwidth for non-stop streaming.
  • Mobile device come with multiple standards and multiple compatibilities
  • Multi-functional features of mobile devices offer several notifications (emails, texts, and calls) which can be a source of distraction (Alshahrani, Ally, 2016).
