The Methodology

Rationale for Methodology

The study has utilized a mixed-method design. The quantitative data was necessary to gather some information about the current use, perception, and adoption of mobile-assisted language learning tools. These findings offered a better understanding of the explored topic. Qualitative data was obtained from the open-ended questions on the survey to further investigate the results of the survey.

The data analysis includes both descriptive and the inferential statistics. The quantitative data obtained from the survey was analyzed, and tables and graphs that illustrate responses on each question and each factor were produced by Google Forms software.

Qualitative information was gained to investigate the results of the survey furthur and to create other possible or deeper themes that could not be captured from the quantitative questions on the survey.
