Participants

This study was conducted at Phillipps University of Marburg (German: Philipps-Universität Marburg) in Marburg, Germany. The university now has about 25,000 students- 12 percent from all over the world, and making Marburg a university town (Philipps-Universität Marburg). A total of 26 participants responded to the email that contained the link to the survey. Respondents ranged in age from 18 to 41. The sample of respondents contains 3 (11.5%) males and 22 (84.6%) females, which is representative of the population. One person (3.8%) chose “no response” as an answer to the gender question.

The undergraduate students are enrolled in the English Teacher Training program, and the graduate degree students are enrolled in Linguistics & Web Technology program.
