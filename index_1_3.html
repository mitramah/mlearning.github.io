Limitations

While this study is intended to perform a survey based on the university where the majority of the students are non-native speakers of English, it might be hard to find potential candidates who specifically study English as a foreign language. Therefore, the study is limited to the group of undergraduate students of the English Teacher program and some graduate students of M.A. Linguistics & Web Technology at Marburg University. The students are native speakers of German, and they are advanced-level English speakers. The study intended to focus on “digital natives” to ensure that participants had exposure to and were familiar with the features of mobile devices. However, the results might merely be representative of the university where the participants are located.
