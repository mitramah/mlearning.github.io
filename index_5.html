Web 2.0 Tools & MALL

(Bicen, 2015) argues that distance education students that use Web 2.0 tools learn more efficiently due to the tools support information-sharing among students and their counterparts. Social networking sites have also continued to grow over time, both in terms of development and number of users. In following pages, the focus is on a few popular Web 2.0 tools in the field of mobile-assisted language learning.
