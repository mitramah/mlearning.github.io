Data Collection Procedures

This survey was designed to reveal the attitudes and perceptions of university students towards the use of mobile-assisted language learning technology to fulfill the educational goals. The descriptive statistics were analyzed. Furthermore, the open-ended questions were analyzed to understand the results of the qualitative data more in depth.

The results of this survey are reported in the following section: The method, The goal, The demographic characteristics of the students, and The survey. Analysis and results of the survey are reported in the following section.


The quantitative data for this study was collected through a web-based survey that was sent via to a group of university students. Qualitative data was collected through an open-ended question on the survey instruments.
